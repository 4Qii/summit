ABOUT US

“Summit Features Sdn. Bhd. (SFSB) is a leading local technology-based company speacializing in motion tracking solutions for life science applications and the creative industry. Our solutions came to elevate the standard of medical and rehabilitation services through the adoption of cutting-edge technology.”

Our Goal

VISION

To be a sustainable leader in scientific fields related to human movement and performance studies to contribute to nation’s growth and building towards year 2030

MISSION

Develop and empower local expertise together with local higher education institutions as catalyst to enhance Malaysia achievement globally in areas such as sports, medical, industry, education and etc.

OUR STORY

Summit Features Sdn Bhd (SFSB) is a bumiputera company registered in the Malaysian Ministry of Finance. SFSB has been operating since 2005. SFSB has ventured into the field of biomechanics and human performance fields since 2007 and has become the main industry support to the institution in the implementation of Motion Analysis in Malaysia.

ENTERTAINMENT

Vicon Virtual Production. Film and Television. Games Brough to Life. Motion capture tools for VFX, gaming production studios and universities

LIFE SCIENCES

Sport performance & biomechanics. Gait Analysis, Neuroscience & Motor Control. Animal Science. Movement analysis tools for biomechanics research, labs and hospital.

ENGINEERING

Robotics & UAVS. Aerospace & Automotive Innovation. Location tracking for advance engineering application.

VIRTUAL PRODUCTION

VR Scouting. Full Body Capture. LED / Green Screen. In Camera VFX. Ergonomic studies for augmented reality labs.
